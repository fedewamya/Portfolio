  1. Tyler Joseph - Drawing of frontman Tyler Joseph from the band Twenty One Pilots. I like drawing tyler because his face structure is nice. Done for my personal exploration project in digital media and design. Wanted to do something differet by adding more eyes and having no pupils. With this I also wanted to work on making the hair look more realistic while still having my stylized pieces.  
  2. Jesse Cale - This is a drawing of one of my favorite poets/musicians, Jesse Cale. This was a personal project I did at home before attending one of his shows where I gave it to him. With this I decided to not blend the shading and just have the colors sit. I did this just to switch it up a little. 
  3. How to solve a Rubiks cube with Spiderman - This is a cover for a book we were making in class explaining to do something. I wanted to illistrate spiderman learning to solve a rubiks cube but instead just getting angry. I really like how it turned out and was happy with all the lines (which is what the assignment was teaching us to do.)
