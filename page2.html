Best work of the 2017-2018 school year

PreviousNext
  1. Tyler Joseph - Drawing of frontman Tyler Joseph from the band Twenty One Pilots. I like drawing tyler because his face structure is nice. Done for my personal exploration project in digital media and design. Wanted to do something differet by adding more eyes and having no pupils. With this I also wanted to work on making the hair look more realistic while still having my stylized pieces.  
  2. Jesse Cale - This is a drawing of one of my favorite poets/musicians, Jesse Cale. This was a personal project I did at home before attending one of his shows where I gave it to him. With this I decided to not blend the shading and just have the colors sit. I did this just to switch it up a little. 
  3. How to solve a Rubiks cube with Spiderman - This is a cover for a book we were making in class explaining to do something. I wanted to illistrate spiderman learning to solve a rubiks cube but instead just getting angry. I really like how it turned out and was happy with all the lines (which is what the assignment was teaching us to do.)

  1. Love Flowers - This drawing was done for an assignment where we had to illistrate flowers in love. I included this because in one short class period I came up with an idea and got it on paper. It could be better quality, but I can always go back and refine it.  
  2. Traditional Flower Tattoo- I drew this right after doing my job shadow at a tattoo parlor. The tattoo artist I observed said if I put in enough work, I could easily do tattoos if I wanted. So I started looking at traditional tattoos, which I really like the style of, and drew a variation of my own. 
  3. Mad Lib - This was drawn for an assignment where the whole class gave adjectives, the words were shuffled and we drew the variation of whatever we got. I got the bussiness of "Small frog weed shop". For a one class period assignment, I think I did a good job, but the lines could be a lot more clean.

Drawings/photoshop pieces done for 102 Design in the 2017-2018 school year.

1

  1. The Story of Us - This was created in photoshop to work on text styles. The Story of Us is an album by one of my favorite artists, Quinn XCII. It included all the songs on the album and a color pallet of the album. Looking back I wish I would have picked colors that fit better together or at least changed the color of the background. 
  2. Aztec Tiles - Aztec tiles is one of the first assignments I remember doing in this class. We drew inspiration from pictures of aztec blankets off of google. I really like the color pallet I choose to use for the final product. 
  3. Seed Bag - Given the assignment to make a seed bag for somwthing that does not actually grow out of the ground, I decided to illistrate growing candy corn. It was around the time of halloween and the debate whether candy corn was good or not was resurfacing. I thought it would be a funny idea to draw it. I like how it turned out, but it could be way cooler if I spent more time on it.
